Science Communication

Science, Technology, Engineering and Maths are for everyone, and while it can take years to conduct research the concepts should be accessible to all. For that reason, I write science articles for kids, give talks in schools, organise outreach events, tell jokes, and founded Neuromatch for kids .
Also, pretty much if you offer me a complimentary mug I’ll happily give a Science talk.
